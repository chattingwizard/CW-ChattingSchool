# Chatting School — Curriculum Overview

## How This School Works

This training is divided into 5 levels. Each level builds on the previous one.

**You must complete each level before moving to the next.**

| Level | Name | Who It's For | Modules |
|-------|------|-------------|---------|
| 1 | Foundations | Brand new recruits | 5 modules |
| 2 | Core Skills | After first week | 5 modules |
| 3 | Sales & Strategy | After first month | 5 modules |
| 4 | Advanced | Experienced chatters | 5 modules |
| 5 | Mastery | Top performers / future TLs | 5 modules |

## Level 1: Foundations
- 1.1 — What is OnlyFans?
- 1.2 — What does a chatter do?
- 1.3 — Your tools: Infloww, Slack, and schedules
- 1.4 — Rules and expectations
- 1.5 — Understanding your KPIs

## Level 2: Core Skills
- 2.1 — How to talk to fans (tone and personality)
- 2.2 — Building rapport
- 2.3 — The message flow: welcome to sale
- 2.4 — PPV basics: what, when, and how much
- 2.5 — Speed and multitasking

## Level 3: Sales & Strategy
- 3.1 — The script framework
- 3.2 — PPV pricing and upselling
- 3.3 — Fan types and how to handle each
- 3.4 — Teasing and escalation
- 3.5 — Custom content sales

## Level 4: Advanced
- 4.1 — Psychology of selling
- 4.2 — Objection handling (OBJ/RES/SIT)
- 4.3 — Difficult situations
- 4.4 — KPI optimization deep dive
- 4.5 — Preparing for leadership

## Level 5: Mastery
- 5.1 — Creating model personas
- 5.2 — Adapting across models
- 5.3 — Revenue maximization
- 5.4 — Quality control and self-review
- 5.5 — Mentoring and coaching others

---

## Assessment
Each level ends with a practical test:
- Level 1: Written quiz
- Level 2: Simulated fan conversation
- Level 3: Live sales scenario
- Level 4: Objection handling roleplay
- Level 5: Create a training module for others
