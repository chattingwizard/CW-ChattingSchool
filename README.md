# CW Chatting School

Training school for Chatting Wizard chatters. From zero to expert.

## Structure

```
curriculum/          → Training modules (Markdown)
docs/                → Web deployment (GitHub Pages)
cursor_context/      → Context for Cursor agent
.cursor/rules/       → Agent rules
```

## Curriculum Plan

### Level 1: Foundations (New Recruits)
- What is OnlyFans and how it works
- The role of a chatter
- Tools overview (Infloww, Slack, schedules)
- Basic rules and expectations
- KPIs explained: what they mean and why they matter

### Level 2: Core Skills
- How to talk to fans (tone, personality, timing)
- Building rapport and keeping fans engaged
- The message flow: from welcome to sale
- PPV basics: what to send and when
- Reply time and multitasking

### Level 3: Sales & Strategy
- Script framework deep dive
- PPV pricing ladder and upselling
- Fan segmentation (new subs, regulars, whales)
- Teasing and escalation techniques
- Custom content sales

### Level 4: Advanced
- Psychology of selling (arousal, ego, scarcity)
- Objection handling (OBJ/RES/SIT system)
- Difficult situations (chargebacks, rude fans, boundaries)
- Advanced KPI optimization
- Coaching others / TL preparation

### Level 5: Mastery
- Model persona creation
- Cross-model adaptation
- Revenue maximization strategies
- Quality control and self-review
- Leadership and mentoring
