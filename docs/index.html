‚è±Ô∏è The 48-Hour Rule: New Subs vs Time Wasters
15 min Coming Soon ‚ñ∂

This is one of the most important strategic concepts you'll learn. It changes how you think about EVERY fan.

The core idea

  • The first 48-72 hours after a fan subscribes is the most valuable window. This is when they're most likely to buy.
  • Our entire Welcome Journey is designed to complete a full sexting session with new fans ‚Äî that's the #1 goal
  • Why? Completing a sexting session creates a powerful emotional connection. The fan feels "we finished together." This drives retention and repeat purchases.
  • Welcome Journey prices are intentionally lower than normal. We prioritize CONVERSION over max revenue per sale.

New Sub (first 48-72h) ‚Äî what you do

  • Follow the Welcome Journey scripts aggressively ‚Äî don't let rapport drag on too long
  • Your GOAL: get this fan through a complete sexting session
  • Use the lower welcome prices ‚Äî they're designed this way on purpose
  • After the sexting: invest HEAVILY in aftercare. This is where you build the connection that makes them come back
  • The aftercare after a FIRST sexting is more important than after a 10th sexting

Time Waster (48-72h+, $0 spent) ‚Äî what you do

  • If a fan has been subscribed 48-72+ hours and hasn't spent anything ‚Üí they're a Time Waster
  • STOP trying to push the standard sexting journey ‚Äî it's not working
  • Switch to bundle/promotion selling: special deals, discounted packages, limited-time offers
  • Lower prices if needed ‚Äî some revenue is better than zero revenue
  • Be creative with offers, but don't waste excessive time ‚Äî these fans are lowest priority

Practice

  • Look at 5 fans in Infloww ‚Üí check their subscription date and total spent ‚Üí classify each as "New Sub" or "Time Waster" ‚Üí explain what approach you'd use for each

  • When to pitch: during Aftercare, when a fan mentions a fantasy, or when they ask directly
  • The TPDs method (Teasing ‚Üí Price ‚Üí Distraction Shift):
  • T ‚Äî Teasing: Send a hot/teasing message to build excitement before the price ("Mmm I'd love to make that just for you‚Ä¶ imagine me doing exactly what you want")
  • P ‚Äî Price: Drop the price while the fan is excited ("That would be $150 babe, and I'll make it so worth it")
  • Ds ‚Äî Distraction Shift: Immediately ask a question to redirect attention ("So tell me‚Ä¶ what would you want me to wear?")
  • Pricing: solo photos from $50+, solo videos from $150‚Äì$200+, special requests higher

Key rules

  • NEVER promise content the model hasn't agreed to produce
  • NEVER give exact delivery time ‚Äî say "I'll make it as soon as I can, babe"
  • ALWAYS collect payment BEFORE the custom is made
  • Log all custom orders in fan notes for TL coordination
