Global Rules (Read First)
1. Linear Flow ‚Äî Once training starts, no breaks until the exam. Exception: pause if next day is Saturday; resume Monday.
2. Fast Expulsion ‚Äî Any recruit who fails to apply instructions, doesn't improve with coaching, or breaks standards ‚Üí removed immediately.
3. Voice Rule ‚Äî During live sessions, microphones MUST remain open. Not interacting verbally = negative evaluation.
4. One-by-One Practice ‚Äî During live practice, one recruit chats at a time. Others MUST watch closely and interact with HM when asked.
5. Self-Study First ‚Äî Recruits study Chatting School content BEFORE each live session. Live sessions are for practice, Q&A, and assessment ‚Äî not lectures.
6. Handovers ‚Äî When ownership changes (HM ‚Üí TL), communicate clearly in Telegram with full context.
7. Model Prep ‚Äî Before each training group, verify the training model has ALL current scripts loaded from the Script Dashboard. If scripts are missing, coordinate with Script Manager before starting.

DAY 0

Interview

Objective

Identify candidates with high proactivity, clear goals, A-player potential, and adequate English level. Verify they actually studied the School content.

Interview Structure (15 min)

  • "Tell me about yourself" (3 min): Background, experience, personal goals. This is the most revealing question ‚Äî let them talk.
  • Motivation Check (3 min): Why do you want this job? What are your goals? What do you know about OnlyFans chatting?
  • Knowledge Check (7 min): What is OnlyFans? What does a chatter do? Name 3 banned topics. What is a PPV? What's the difference between a new sub and a time waster? What is the 48-hour rule?
  • Quick Writing Test (2 min): "Write me a short flirty message as if you were a model responding to a fan who said 'Hey beautiful'." ‚Äî Evaluates English level AND personality in one go.

Pass/Fail Criteria

  • ‚úÖ Pass: Motivated, background shows initiative, answered knowledge questions correctly, writing test shows B1+ English and personality
  • ‚ùå Fail: Can't answer basics (didn't study), writing test shows poor English or zero personality, unclear motivation, red flags in background

DAY 2

The Journey Step by Step (Live Workshop)

Objective

Walk through every journey phase with real examples. Practice at each step. Ensure recruits understand branch rules and NR waves.

Session Agenda (~2-3 hours)

  • Day 2 Review Quiz (5 min): Ask verbally. "What's the 48-hour rule? What does /n activate? Where's the model profile?"
  • Annotated Chat Walkthrough (30 min): Show 2-3 full real conversations on screen. Annotate each phase. Ask recruits to identify: "What phase is this? What script is being used?"
  • Rapport + Teasing Practice (20 min): One-by-one: recruit writes Rapport and Teasing messages in-character. HM gives feedback on tone, length, timing.
  • Sexting + PPV Drops (25 min): Walk through sexting sequence. Explain welcome pricing (intentionally lower). Practice writing sexting messages.
  • Aftercare Deep Dive (10 min): Emphasize: aftercare after FIRST sexting is the most important. Practice writing 3 aftercare messages.
  • Branch Rules + NR Waves (20 min): Show the flowchart. Quiz: "Fan went silent after PPV. What do you do?" Go through all 5 NR waves.
  • Fan Assessment Practice (15 min): Show 6 fan profiles ‚Üí recruits rank priority ‚Üí explain reasoning. Verify they use the 48-hour rule.
HM Assessment Checklist ‚Äî Day 2
Recruit can identify all journey phases in a real conversation
Recruit writes in-character messages that match the model's voice
Recruit knows the 3 branch rules (bought / silent / objected)
Recruit can prioritize fans correctly (New Sub > Time Waster)
Recruit understands why welcome prices are lower

DAY 3

Advanced Skills + Full Simulation (Live)

Objective

Master objections, customs (TPDs), multitasking. Complete a full end-to-end conversation simulation. This is the make-or-break day.

Session Agenda (~3 hours)

  • Day 3 Review Quiz (5 min): "Fan says 'that's too expensive.' Which script? What are the 5 NR waves? After a first sexting, what's most important?"
  • Top 10 Objections Drill (25 min): For each objection: HM says the fan's line ‚Üí recruit must respond with the correct script in-character. Fast-paced, no hesitation allowed.
  • Custom Sales TPDs Practice (15 min): HM plays a fan asking for a custom ‚Üí recruit must execute full TPDs (Teasing ‚Üí Price ‚Üí Distraction Shift). Practice with 2-3 scenarios.
  • Multitasking Scenario (15 min): Present 4 fans messaging at once. Recruit must: rank priority, write first response to each, explain reasoning.
  • Shift Routine Review (10 min): Walk through start checklist + handoff checklist. Emphasize: "the next chatter should pick up without asking questions."
  • Full Simulation (45 min, one-by-one): Each recruit does a complete conversation: New sub ‚Üí Rapport ‚Üí Teasing ‚Üí Sexting ‚Üí PPV ‚Üí handle objection ‚Üí NR wave ‚Üí Aftercare ‚Üí Custom pitch. HM plays the fan. Detailed feedback after each.

DAY 4

Final Exam

Objective

Validate comprehensive knowledge and ability to execute independently. Pass/fail gate before supervised shift.

Part 1: Written Quiz (15 min)

  • 20 questions covering all previous sections' content
  • Topics: banned topics, journey phases, script selection, branch rules, fan prioritization, TPDs, pricing, shift routine
  • Pass: 80% (16/20 correct)
  • If fail: can retake ONCE after 30 min review. Second fail = removed.

Part 2: Live Chatting Exam (1-2 hours)

  • Recruit chats in Infloww unassisted ‚Äî handle real fans independently
  • HM observes but provides ZERO input, coaching, or intervention
  • Scoring rubric (each 1-5):
  • ‚Äî Script adherence: Did they use scripts correctly?
  • ‚Äî Character consistency: Did they stay in-model?
  • ‚Äî Fan assessment: Did they prioritize correctly?
  • ‚Äî Response speed: Under 2 minutes?
  • ‚Äî Problem solving: How did they handle unexpected situations?
  • Pass: Average score 3.5/5 or higher, no category below 2

14 DAYS

Probation Period

Ownership Transfer: Mileh ‚Üí Rycel + TL

After the exam, Mileh hands off to Rycel (Chatter Manager) who assigns the recruit to a Team Leader. From this point, the TL owns the recruit's development.

  • Mileh sends handover to Rycel via Telegram with: recruit name, exam scores, strengths, areas to watch
  • Rycel assigns to TL based on shift availability and team balance
  • TL takes over: coaching, monitoring, and pass/fail decision

Week 1: Close Monitoring

  • TL assigns 1-2 models (reduced workload)
  • TL reviews chats daily in the first 3 days
  • Coaching sessions: Day 2, Day 4, Day 6 of probation
  • Focus: script adherence, character consistency, reply speed
  • Recruit begins Ongoing Development modules (Week 2: Advanced Objections)
  • Red flag triggers: consistent reply time >3 min, breaking character, ignoring scripts, banned topic incident

Week 2: Building Independence

  • TL increases workload to normal (full model assignments)
  • Coaching sessions: Day 8, Day 10, Day 12 of probation
  • KPI tracking begins: Sales/hr, Messages/hr, Reply Time, CVR
  • Focus: consistency, sales improvement, handling difficult situations
  • Recruit continues Ongoing Development modules

Day 14: Pass/Fail Decision

  • TL submits probation report to Rycel with: KPI averages, quality assessment, improvement trajectory, recommendation
  • Pass criteria:
  • ‚Äî Sales/hr ‚â• $20 (or clear upward trend)
  • ‚Äî Reply time consistently < 2 min
  • ‚Äî No banned topic violations
  • ‚Äî Script adherence ‚â• 70%
  • ‚Äî Positive improvement trajectory
  • Fail criteria:
  • ‚Äî Sales/hr < $15 with no improvement
  • ‚Äî Repeated character breaks
  • ‚Äî Ignoring TL coaching
  • ‚Äî Banned topic violations
  • Rycel makes final call. If edge case ‚Üí extend probation 7 days.
